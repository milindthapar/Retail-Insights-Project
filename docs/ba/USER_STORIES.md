# User Stories – Retail Revenue Insights Platform

This file contains user stories for the Retail Revenue Insights project.  
All stories follow the Agile format: *As a [user], I want [need], so that [business value].*

---

## 1. Sales Director – Executive Overview
**As a** Sales Director  
**I want** a dashboard that shows revenue, profit, and margin at a glance  
**so that** I can understand overall performance without digging through files.

---

## 2. Regional Manager – Geographic Insights
**As a** Regional Manager  
**I want** to compare performance across regions and states  
**so that** I can identify high-performing territories and areas that need interventions.

---

## 3. Finance Manager – Discount Impact
**As a** Finance Manager  
**I want** visibility into how discounts affect profitability  
**so that** I can limit unnecessary discounting and protect operating margin.

---

## 4. Marketing Lead – Customer Segmentation
**As a** Marketing Lead  
**I want** customers segmented by spending behaviour (RFM/risk/value)  
**so that** I can plan better retention, cross-sell, and loyalty campaigns.

---

## 5. Supply Chain Lead – Demand Forecasting
**As a** Supply Chain Lead  
**I want** a forward-looking sales forecast  
**so that** inventory and procurement can be planned more accurately.

---

## 6. BI Analyst – Automated Refresh
**As a** BI Analyst  
**I want** the dataset to refresh automatically from a cloud database  
**so that** I don’t need to manually clean and upload Excel/CSV files every day.

---

## 7. CEO – Product Profitability
**As a** CEO  
**I want** a clear view of product-level profit and loss  
**so that** I can make decisions on discontinuing low-margin SKUs.

---

## 8. Customer Experience Manager – Shipping Behaviour
**As a** CX Manager  
**I want** to analyse shipping modes and delivery patterns  
**so that** I can improve customer satisfaction and logistics efficiency.

---

### Total stories: 8  
(Strong coverage across Sales, Finance, Marketing, BI, Supply Chain, and Executive leadership.)
